# Store (overview)

The VisuPair **store** sells original work: garments, accessories, paintings, artworks, and digital pieces—designed and produced by the studio. Some items are physical, some digital, some both; many releases are **limited** or **one-of-a-kind**.

## Store sections (routes)

Typical category hubs include:

- **Garments / fashion design** — e.g. `/store/fashion-design` (see live navigation)
- **3D models** — e.g. `/store/3d-models`
- **Artworks** — e.g. `/store/artworks`

Exact paths and inventory change over time. **Authoritative product data** (price, stock, variants, shipping) is only on the live store and checkout—not in this static mirror.
