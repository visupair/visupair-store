# About VisuPair

**Gul** and **Vali** are multidisciplinary designers and co-founders. VisuPair is where they collaborate after years of making work they care about—*VisuPair is where all of it comes together.*

## Background

- Traditional **painting** (oils, light, texture, color) remains foundational.
- **Digital design** expanded the practice—visuals, technology, and building things that did not exist before.
- **Fashion** followed as a natural step: clothing as wearable art.

## Phygital creation

The studio designs in 3D, builds digital patterns, and produces real garments and objects. The same ideas can exist as **3D models**, **wearable avatars**, or **digital collectibles**. **Phygital** means moving an idea between screen and material (and back) without losing what makes it special.

## Collaboration with clients

VisuPair works with **artists, independent designers, and brands**. Offerings include logos, websites, color palettes, creative direction, lookbooks, and full visual identities—with emphasis on presence that reflects the work honestly, including for people who find it hard to present work without feeling over-produced.

## Workshops and teaching

Workshops and courses cover areas such as **oil painting**, **digital design**, and **3D & phygital creation**, plus relaxed **art nights**. Guest teachers are invited alongside in-house sessions; learning is meant to be honest, open, and worth participants’ time.
