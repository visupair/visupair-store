# Welcome to VisuPair

**Visupair** is a creative platform: art, fashion, and digital design in one place. Positioning: *we didn’t start a business—we just never stopped making.*

## What you’ll find here

- **Store** — Original garments, accessories, paintings, artworks, and digital pieces (availability varies; many pieces are limited or one-of-a-kind).
- **Portfolio** — Selected studio and project work across design, 3D, fashion, and digital craft.
- **Services** — Visual identity, web, creative direction, and related work for artists, designers, and brands.
- **Courses** — Workshops and courses (topics include oil painting, digital design, and 3D / phygital work).
- **About** — Story of the studio and founders Gul and Vali.
- **Support** — FAQ, order help, and policies.

## Phygital (quick definition)

**Phygital** describes work that exists in both physical and digital form—for example, a garment you can wear and a matching 3D model or digital collectible.
