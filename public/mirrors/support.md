# Support & FAQ (VisuPair)

**Purpose:** Help with orders, products, and services. Below is a plain-text summary of common questions. For the full interactive page, use the [Support page](https://visupair.com/support).

## What exactly is VisuPair?

VisuPair is a creative studio run by two multidisciplinary designers. They create their own art, fashion, and digital pieces, collaborate with artists and brands on visual identity, and teach workshops.

## What does “phygital” mean?

Phygital describes work that exists in both the physical and digital world. A garment can be worn in real life and also exist as a 3D model or digital collectible. The studio moves ideas between both without losing meaning.

## What is a digital collectible?

A piece that exists only digitally—e.g. a wearable for an avatar, a 3D model, or digital artwork. It is real creative work without a physical object.

## What kind of pieces do you sell?

Garments, accessories, paintings, artworks, and digital works, designed and made by the studio. Some pieces are physical only, digital only, or both. Some are one-of-a-kind; others are very limited. Availability changes by release.

## What kind of clients do you work with?

Artists, independent designers, and brands of all sizes—worldwide, often **fully remote**—when they need help presenting creative work in a way that feels true.

## What services do you offer?

Logos, visual identities, websites, color palettes, creative direction, lookbooks, and more. Projects start with a conversation to understand what is needed.

## Do you only run your own workshops?

No. Guest creators, artists, and designers are invited to teach. The same values apply whether sessions are in-house or guest-led.

## How do I start working with you?

Reach out with a short description of the project; the team follows up to schedule a conversation.

## How do I track my orders?

Log in and open **Purchases** for status, tracking (when available), delivery estimates, and courier details. Tracking emails are also sent.

## Can I return or refund orders?

Eligibility depends on product type and condition. See the [Refund Policy](https://visupair.com/legal/refund-policy). For a specific order: **info@visupair.com**.
